Our Story

Founded in 2020, CleanPro started with a simple mission: to make professional cleaning services accessible, reliable, and affordable for everyone. What began as a small team of dedicated cleaners has grown into a trusted platform serving thousands of satisfied customers.

We believe that a clean space is essential for a healthy, productive, and happy life. That's why we've assembled a team of trained professionals who use eco-friendly products and modern equipment to deliver exceptional results every time.
